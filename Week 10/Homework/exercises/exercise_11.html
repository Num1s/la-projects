Avatar is a sci-fi film directed by James Cameron. It’s set on Pandora, a lush alien moon with unique flora and fauna. The story follows Jake Sully, a paraplegic marine, who joins the Avatar Program. Using an alien body, Jake interacts with the Na’vi, Pandora’s native people. He learns their ways and grows close to Neytiri, a Na’vi warrior. The conflict arises when humans try to exploit Pandora’s resources. Jake sides with the Na’vi to protect their home. The movie is known for its groundbreaking visuals and 3D effects. It explores themes of environmentalism and cultural respect. Avatar became one of the highest-grossing films in history.
